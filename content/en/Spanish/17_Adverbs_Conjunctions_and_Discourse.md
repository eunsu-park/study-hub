## 2. Adverbs of Place

| Spanish | English | Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| aquí / acá | here | dentro | inside |
| ahí | there (near you) | fuera | outside |
| allí / allá | there (far) | cerca | near |
| arriba | up, upstairs | lejos | far |
| abajo | down, downstairs | enfrente | opposite |
| adelante | forward | alrededor | around |
| atrás | behind, back | | |

> **Aquí/acá and allí/allá**: *aquí/allí* are more specific locations;
> *acá/allá* suggest general direction. Latin America uses *acá/allá* more.

## 4. Adverbs of Quantity

| Spanish | English | Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| muy | very | más | more |
| mucho | much, a lot | menos | less |
| poco | little | casi | almost |
| bastante | quite, enough | apenas | barely |
| demasiado | too much | nada | not at all |
| tan | so | tanto | so much |

> **Muy vs. mucho**: *Muy* modifies adjectives/adverbs; *mucho* modifies verbs.
> - Es **muy** alto. (He's very tall.)
> - Trabaja **mucho**. (He works a lot.)

## 5. -MENTE Adverb Formation

### Rule: Feminine form of adjective + -mente

| Masculine Adj. | Feminine Adj. | Adverb | English |
|---------------|--------------|--------|---------|
| rápido | rápid**a** | rápidamente | quickly |
| lento | lent**a** | lentamente | slowly |
| tranquilo | tranquil**a** | tranquilamente | calmly |
| perfecto | perfect**a** | perfectamente | perfectly |

### Adjectives ending in -e or consonant: Add -mente directly

| Adjective | Adverb | English |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| fácil | fácil**mente** | easily |
| frecuente | frecuente**mente** | frequently |
| feliz | feliz**mente** | happily |
| normal | normal**mente** | normally |

### Two Consecutive -mente Adverbs

The first adverb uses the **adjective form only**; only the last gets -mente:
- Habla **clara** y **lentamente**. (He speaks clearly and slowly.)
- Lo hizo **rápida** y **eficientemente**. (She did it quickly and efficiently.)

## 6. Coordinating Conjunctions

| Conjunction | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **y** | and | Juan **y** María |
| **e** | and (before i-/hi-) | padre **e** hijo |
| **o** | or | ¿café **o** té? |
| **u** | or (before o-/ho-) | siete **u** ocho |
| **pero** | but | Es caro, **pero** bueno. |
| **sino** | but rather (after negation) | No es rojo, **sino** azul. |
| **ni... ni** | neither... nor | **Ni** café **ni** té. |

> **Pero vs. sino**: Use *sino* only after a negative statement to introduce
> a contradicting alternative:
> - No quiero café **sino** té. (I don't want coffee but rather tea.)

---

## 7. Subordinating Conjunctions

| Conjunction | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **porque** | because | Estudio **porque** quiero aprender. |
| **ya que / puesto que** | since | **Ya que** llueve, me quedo. |
| **aunque** | although | **Aunque** es caro, lo compro. |
| **mientras** | while | Leo **mientras** como. |
| **cuando** | when | Llámame **cuando** llegues. |
| **si** | if | **Si** llueve, no voy. |
| **para que** | so that (+subj.) | Hablo despacio **para que** entiendas. |
| **antes de que** | before (+subj.) | Sal **antes de que** llueva. |
| **después de que** | after | **Después de que** comimos, salimos. |
| **como** | as, since | **Como** no tengo dinero, no voy. |

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|
| Por ejemplo | For example |
| Es decir | That is |
| O sea | In other words |
| En otras palabras | In other words |

| Cause | Effect |
|-------|--------|

## 11. Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1: Form -mente Adverbs

1. tranquilo → ________
2. fácil → ________
3. rápido → ________
4. probable → ________
5. "clearly and slowly" (two adverbs) → ________

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. tranquilamente 2. fácilmente 3. rápidamente 4. probablemente
5. clara y lentamente (first uses adjective form only)

</details>

### Exercise 2: Choose the Correct Conjunction

1. No quiero café ________ té. (but rather)
2. Estudio ________ quiero aprobar. (because)
3. Son las siete ________ ocho. (or → before o-)
4. Hablo despacio ________ me entiendas. (so that)

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. **sino** 2. **porque** 3. **u** 4. **para que** (+ subjunctive)

</details>

### Exercise 3: Add Discourse Markers

Improve this text by adding appropriate markers.

"Viajé a España. Visité Madrid. Fui a Barcelona. Me gustó mucho Barcelona. Fue un viaje increíble."

<details>
<summary>Sample Answer</summary>

"**El verano pasado**, viajé a España. **En primer lugar**, visité Madrid.
**Después**, fui a Barcelona. **Sin embargo**, me gustó mucho **más** Barcelona.
**En definitiva**, fue un viaje increíble."

</details>
