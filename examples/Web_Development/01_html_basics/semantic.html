Semantic vs Non-semantic Comparison

Non-semantic (bad example)

<div id="header">...</div>
<div id="nav">...</div>
<div id="main">
    <div class="article">...</div>
</div>
<div id="footer">...</div>

Semantic (good example)

<header>...</header>
<nav>...</nav>
<main>
    <article>...</article>
</main>
<footer>...</footer>
